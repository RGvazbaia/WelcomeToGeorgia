Giorgi Nabakhteveli

Hello, I am Giorgi Nabakhteveli. My hobbies are travel, hiking, mountaineering, history and geography. I am currently working as a hiking and cultural guide. I will be happy to help you in finding interesting and hard-to-reach places and planning your trip in Georgia.

Inga Shermandini

Hello, I am Inga and I was born and raised in Tbilisi. My "mission" is to show travelers my city from a local's perspective, to share my passion and enthusiasm for my hometown. I like to focus on Georgian traditions, customs and way of life today and in the past to give you an idea of what it feels like to be a local in Tbilisi.
